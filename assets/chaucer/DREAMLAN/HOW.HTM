HOW THE RIDES WORK AT DREAMLAND, MARGATE

Enterprise

The Enterprise is a wheel with 23 carriages on it which are capable of holding two people in each one. The wheel starts to spin and the carriages are pulled out by a centrifugal force, then the wheel is lifted out by a hydraulic ram and the wheel is then spinning horizontally!

Wild Mouse

The Wild Mouse has eight cars which can carry up to four people at the same time. The cars are pulled up the big steep at the first bend and is taken around the rest of ride by gravity


Log Drop

There are eight logs on the Log Drop. The logs travel along with the current of the water, when they reach the "upward slopes," it is pulled up on convarer belt, and it comes back down the water slide with gravity and has a lubriction of water. As the log hits the bottom the water slows the log down and creates a big splash at the same time.
