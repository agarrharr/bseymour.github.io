WELCOME TO THE
RELIGIOUS EDUCATION  DEPARTMENT
 
 

The Religious Education Department at Charters School is a lively and innovative department. It is   staffed by teachers that are successful and experienced at teaching Religious Education at each level in a secondary school. A dedicated suite of classrooms ensure that we have the environment and resources that ensure that a high quality educational experience is on offer to each of our pupils. Courses on offer range from those in Years 7,8 & 9, to our popular GCSE course in years 10 and 11 and finally to our exciting and challenging A Level.
